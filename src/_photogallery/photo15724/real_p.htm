Johann Franz Sammet (Francis Summit, Sr.)

Cemetery located near Cordova, North Carolina. Reads Hir ligt begraben Frantz Sammet er ist gebohren im Jahr 1741 im Nornung und ist gestorgen den 27 Hornung Anno 1818.

Here's the literal translation of the German text as provided by a professor from the German Department at Catholic University in Washington, D.C.

Here lies buried Frantz Sammet. He was born in the year 1741 in February and he died on 27 February in the year 1818.
